My Top 3 Movies of all time

The silence of the lambs

It's a movie, that captivates me every time, no matter how often I watch it. Anthony Hopkins and Jodie Foster are playing their role absolutely amazing.

What dreams may come

A movie with beautiful pictures full of fantasy an deep life philosophy at the same time. In my opinion one of the greatest roles Robin Williams had ever played.

The last unicorn

A beautiful animated story suitable for all times an ages. The title song makes me cry every time.
